Top Destinations

Ooty

Nestled in the Nilgiri Hills, Ooty is a charming hill station known for its tea gardens, lakes, and beautiful colonial architecture.

Kodaikanal

Popularly known as the Princess of Hill Stations, Kodaikanal offers serene lakes, lush greenery, and scenic viewpoints perfect for nature lovers.

Kanyakumari

Located at the southernmost tip of India, Kanyakumari is famous for its sunrise/sunset views, Vivekananda Rock Memorial, and Triveni Sangam.
